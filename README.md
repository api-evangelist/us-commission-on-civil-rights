# US Commission on Civil Rights (us-commission-on-civil-rights)
The US Commission on Civil Rights is an independent, bipartisan federal agency that investigates, reports on, and issues public service announcements about discrimination or denials of equal protection based on race, color, religion, sex, age, disability, national origin, or in the electoral process.
